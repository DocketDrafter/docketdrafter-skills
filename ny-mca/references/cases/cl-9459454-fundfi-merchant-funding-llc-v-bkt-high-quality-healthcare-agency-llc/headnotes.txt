In a breach of contract action, defendants BKT Quality Healthcare Agency LLC and Kyana E
Hall moved to dismiss for lack of jurisdiction, challenging the enforcement of a forum
selection clause under New York law and asserting forged signatures. The court
considered statutory provisions governing jurisdiction over foreign corporations and the
applicability of the forum selection clause in a case involving less than $1 million. It
found the clause prima facie valid and enforceable, declined to dismiss on forum
grounds, and held dismissal premature regarding forgery allegations due to unresolved
factual disputes requiring further discovery. The motion to dismiss was denied without
prejudice.
