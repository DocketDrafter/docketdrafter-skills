In Champion Auto Sales, LLC v. Pearl Beta Funding, LLC, the Appellate Division, First
Department, reviewed a Supreme Court order granting defendant's motion to dismiss a
complaint seeking to vacate a judgment by confession. The plaintiffs challenged the
underlying agreement as usurious. Applying the standard for usury, the court held that
the evidence did not establish the transaction as usurious and found plaintiffs'
additional challenges unavailing. The court therefore unanimously affirmed the dismissal
of the complaint without costs.
