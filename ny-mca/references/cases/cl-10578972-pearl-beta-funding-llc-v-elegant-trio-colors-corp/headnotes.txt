In a breach of contract action, the plaintiff Pearl Beta Funding, LLC, a Delaware
limited liability company authorized to do business in New York, appealed from an order
and judgment dismissing its complaint against defendants Elegant Trio Colors Corp., a
California corporation, and its owner. The Supreme Court granted the defendants’ CPLR
3211(a)(2) motion to dismiss for lack of subject matter jurisdiction under Business
Corporation Law § 1314. The Appellate Division dismissed the appeal from the order but
reversed the judgment, holding the plaintiff’s affidavits sufficiently alleged contract
performance in New York to establish jurisdiction, and denied the motion to dismiss,
reinstating the complaint.
