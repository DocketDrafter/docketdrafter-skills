In this appeal from an amended judgment awarding plaintiff Bridge Funding Cap LLC
damages for breach of a revenue purchase agreement and guaranty, the Appellate Division,
Fourth Department, reversed the judgment and denied plaintiff’s summary judgment motion.
The dispute involved whether the agreement constituted a lawful revenue purchase
agreement or an unenforceable usurious loan. Applying established factors—presence of
reconciliation provisions, absence of a finite term, and lack of recourse upon
bankruptcy—the court held the agreement was a revenue purchase agreement. However,
plaintiff failed to meet its prima facie burden on damages due to conflicting evidence,
requiring denial of summary judgment. The court’s ruling thus reversed the judgment
without costs.
