In this appeal from a Supreme Court order in Onondaga County denying plaintiff’s motion
for partial summary judgment and granting defendant summary judgment dismissing a breach
of contract and account stated complaint, the appellate court affirmed. The dispute
concerned plaintiff’s standing as an alleged assignee of Discover Bank to recover credit
card debt. Applying the business record exception to hearsay under CPLR 4518(a), the
court held plaintiff failed to provide admissible evidence establishing standing because
the agent’s affidavit lacked personal knowledge of the record-keeping practices and did
not verify that the submitted spreadsheet was a true and accurate representation of
business records.
