In this motion to dismiss, plaintiffs challenged agreements with Arch Capital Funding,
LLC and Cap Call, LLC, alleging usury, RICO violations, and related claims. The court
applied New York law, examining whether the agreements constituted loans subject to
usury statutes or non-loan merchant agreements. It held that the Arch agreements,
featuring reconciliation provisions, evergreen terms, and contingent repayment, were not
loans as a matter of law, dismissing all usury and RICO claims against Arch. However,
the Cap Call agreement lacked a reconciliation clause, precluding dismissal of usury and
related RICO claims against Cap Call at this stage. Several other causes of action were
dismissed for failure to state cognizable claims.
