In this breach of contract action, plaintiff Advance Services Group LLC moved for
summary judgment against defendants Acadian Properties Austin LLC and guarantors Shannon
and Brandon Badeaux, seeking enforcement of a receivable purchase agreement. Defendants
cross-moved for summary judgment dismissing the complaint, arguing the agreement was a
usurious loan rather than a purchase of future receivables. Applying the test from LG
Funding LLC, the court found the agreement created an absolute obligation to repay with
features indicative of a loan, including lack of a true reconciliation provision and
immediate recourse upon bankruptcy, making the agreement usurious. Accordingly, the
court denied plaintiff’s motion, granted defendants’ cross motion, and dismissed the
complaint.
