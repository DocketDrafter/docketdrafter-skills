In an appeal from an order denying summary judgment, the Appellate Division, Second
Department, reversed and granted Apollo Funding Co.'s motion in a breach of contract and
personal guaranty action against Dave Reilly Construction, LLC. The plaintiff purchased
an interest in DRC’s future receivables for $25,000, with repayment tied to a percentage
of monthly sales until $37,750 was collected. The defendants claimed the agreement was a
usurious loan, but the court held it was not a loan since payments varied with receipts,
there was no finite term, and no bankruptcy provision triggered default. The plaintiff
showed prima facie entitlement, and defendants failed to raise triable issues.
