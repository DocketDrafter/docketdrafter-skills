In an appeal from Supreme Court, New York County, the Appellate Division, First
Department, unanimously modified a judgment entered on April 11, 2024, in a special
proceeding under Executive Law § 63(12) alleging respondents engaged in predatory
lending through merchant cash advance agreements (MCAs) that were usurious
loans-in-disguise. The court affirmed respondents’ liability for violations of Executive
Law § 63(12) based on usury, fraud, and unconscionability, finding joint and several
liability appropriate. The monetary judgment was vacated and remanded for further
proceedings to determine restitution amounts without allowing disgorgement or double
recovery, considering offsets and repayment of principal. Appeal of denial to amend
judgment was dismissed as moot.
